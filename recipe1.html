Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Combine the soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a pot.
  2. Simmer the chicken in the sauce for 30-40 minutes.
  3. Once the chicken is tender, allow the sauce to reduce and serve over rice.
